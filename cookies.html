This Cookies Policy explains how Vext Audit Capital, a division of SkyDasher Tech LLP, uses cookies and similar tracking technologies on vextaudit.com.

1. What are cookies

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They allow the website to recognise your device, remember preferences, and collect usage information. Cookies may be set by vextaudit.com directly (first-party) or by third-party services (third-party cookies).

The Firm does not use advertising cookies, retargeting cookies, social media tracking pixels, or any cookie that builds a profile of browsing activity across other websites.

3. Consent — Indian and international visitors

For visitors from India: By continuing to use vextaudit.com after viewing the cookie notice displayed on first visit, you provide implied consent to the use of analytics cookies as described above. Strictly necessary cookies do not require consent.

For visitors from the European Union, United Kingdom, and other jurisdictions where GDPR or equivalent laws apply: Analytics cookies require your explicit opt-in consent before they are set. A cookie consent banner is displayed on first visit requiring you to accept or decline analytics cookies. Your preference is stored for 12 months. You may change your preference at any time by clearing your browser cookies and revisiting the site, or by using the Google Analytics opt-out tool described below.

Consent records for EU/UK visitors include timestamp, IP address (anonymised), and consent choice, and are stored for audit purposes.

4. Managing and withdrawing consent

  • Browser settings: Block or delete cookies through your browser's privacy settings. Blocking strictly necessary cookies will affect website functionality.
  • Google Analytics opt-out: Install the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on at tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout to prevent Google Analytics from collecting your data across all websites.
  • Do Not Track: The Firm respects browser Do Not Track signals by limiting analytics data collection where technically possible.

5. Third-party cookies

Google Analytics is the only third-party service setting cookies on vextaudit.com. Google may process analytics data outside India subject to Google's standard contractual clauses and GDPR adequacy commitments. Review Google's privacy practices at policies.google.com/privacy.
